an ideological imprint

Although propaganda is regarded as a modern term, closely associated with the instances of poster propaganda distributed throughout the West in World War I and World War II, the essence of propaganda can be traced back to the Ancient Civilizations. Propaganda has morphed through many forms, conveying itself through communication systems and mass media technologies concurrent to its day and age. Created with the intent to suppose an idea/ truth consequently shaping social and cultural attitudes, propaganda has the power to subject the masses to a system of belief or framework of thought.
